"Anyone who stops learning is old, whether at 20 or at 80. The greatest thing in life is  to keep your mind young.-Henry Ford"

It should not come as a suprise that in today's world Adaption is the new currency,where new innovations are happening at an outrageous rate which anybody might find overwhelming to keep up with.But with a passion to be a life long learner will not only help us survive in this ever chaning entrepreneurial landscape but to eventually thrive in it.To be a constant learner means not just staying ahead in your own field,but to also develop an interest and curiosit in the general working of the universe. As said , sometimes it can all be a little more than overwhelming witnessing so many changes happening so fast..but forming a habit to be a constant learner doesnt have to be this difficult or time consuming!

But how do one start of to commit to this lifelong goal ? Here are three very refined smart ways to start ...

  • Find the right motivation - being the most important and time consuming of all once established..learning could become the next hobby!
  • Set concrete goals - For the first certain period of time creating objectives would help to stay motivated by seeing a continous improvment in the set tasks
  • Make use of downtime - learning doesn't always have to visual with podcasts and tons of audiobooks available one can make use of for example even their commute time!

I , therefore want to make use of this site to bring togther a number of creational and motivating articles/materials to help me and anybody who wants to embark this new journey of learning!
